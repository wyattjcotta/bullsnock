Installation Guide

1. Download official Bullsnock Launcher here if you haven't already.
2. Open the launcher, click Launch and login to your Minecraft account. The launcher will automatically download and install all necessary files. This could take a few minutes.
3. When the game launches, in the Multiplayer menu, add our server with the adreess:

mc.bullsnock.com
